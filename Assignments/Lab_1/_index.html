About the Club

The VTC Adventures Guild or just The Guild as most members call it, is the Vermont Technical College Randolph campus gaming club. We welcome anyone who wants to play any kind of games, from classic board games and table top role play games to modern video games we play it all and most members are willing to at lest try any game some one wants to play.
